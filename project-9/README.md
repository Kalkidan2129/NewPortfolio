
# Global Fossil Fuel Production and Consumption - Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project created a comprehensive Power BI dashboard presenting detailed insights into global fossil fuel production and consumption patterns. It highlights major consumer countries and the environmental impacts of fossil fuel use. The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making for transitioning to renewable energy sources.

---

## Business Problem

Fossil fuels supply around 80% of the world’s energy but are the largest contributors to global climate change and local air pollution. There is an urgent need to identify key fossil fuel consumers to promote a shift towards low-carbon energy sources. The project addresses the challenge of visualizing complex energy data to inform policy and business strategies.

---

## Objective

- Analyze global fossil fuel production and consumption data
- Identify major fossil fuel consuming countries
- Support transition strategies towards renewable energy

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Excel
- Microsoft Office
- ETL processes
- Data Reporting

---

## Project Workflow

- Collect and clean global fossil fuel production and consumption data
- Perform ETL to prepare data for analysis
- Develop interactive Power BI dashboard visualizations
- Analyze data to identify key fossil fuel consumers and trends
- Present insights to support renewable energy transition decisions

---

## Key Insights

- Fossil fuels remain the dominant energy source globally, supplying about 80% of energy needs
- The last decade was the hottest in 125,000 years, linked to fossil fuel emissions
- Major fossil fuel consumers have significant roles in global climate change
- Transitioning to renewables like wind, solar, and nuclear is critical to reduce pollution and warming
