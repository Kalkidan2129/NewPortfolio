
# Bike Sales in Six Countries (2011 - 2016)

## Project Overview

This project analyzed bicycle sales data from six countries between 2011 and 2016 using Power BI to identify year-to-year sales changes. It involved data modeling, creating DAX measures, and visualizing sales trends to understand seasonal and economic impacts. The insights informed recommendations to improve sales performance across regions.

---

## Business Problem

The bicycle industry faces challenges from seasonal weather variations and economic conditions affecting discretionary spending. The company needed to understand year-over-year sales fluctuations across countries to address declining or inconsistent sales.

---

## Objective

- Identify year-over-year sales changes across six countries
- Visualize sales trends by product category and subcategory
- Provide actionable recommendations to mitigate sales volatility

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Excel
- Microsoft Office
- CSV Data Files

---

## Project Workflow

- Load and clean bike sales CSV data in Power BI
- Create a date table and establish relationships
- Develop DAX measures for sales, YOY%, and gross profit margin
- Build line and stacked column charts by product category
- Analyze visualizations to extract key sales insights

---

## Key Insights

- Bike sales exhibit strong seasonality influenced by weather patterns
- Economic conditions significantly impact discretionary bike purchases
- Year-over-year sales growth varies notably by country and product category
- Late-arriving spring weather correlates with lower sales in some regions
