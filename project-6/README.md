
# Bike Sales in Six Countries (2011 - 2016)

## Project Overview

This project analyzed bicycle sales data from 2011 to 2016 across six countries, focusing on year-over-year sales changes. Using Power BI, it visualized sales performance by age group and identified key factors affecting sales fluctuations. The insights aimed to support strategic decisions to mitigate seasonal and economic impacts on sales.

---

## Business Problem

The bicycle industry faces challenges due to seasonal demand fluctuations and sensitivity to economic conditions, impacting sales performance. Unusual weather patterns and discretionary spending behavior further complicate forecasting and inventory management. The project sought to understand these dynamics to improve sales strategies.

---

## Objective

- Identify year-over-year sales changes across different countries
- Analyze sales performance segmented by customer age groups
- Provide recommendations to address seasonal and economic impacts on sales

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- CSV Data Files
- Microsoft Office Suite

---

## Project Workflow

- Load and clean bike sales data in Power BI
- Create a date table and establish relationships
- Transform and reorder categorical data (age groups)
- Develop DAX measures for sales metrics and YOY calculations
- Design and format visualizations including line and stacked column charts

---

## Key Insights

- Bike sales exhibit strong seasonality influenced by weather conditions
- Economic factors significantly affect discretionary spending on bicycles
- Sales trends vary notably across different countries and age groups
- Year-over-year sales changes highlight periods of growth and decline for targeted intervention
