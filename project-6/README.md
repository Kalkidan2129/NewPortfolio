
# Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive sales dashboard using Power BI to visualize sales data by country, segment, category, and subcategory. The dashboard enables stakeholders to analyze sales trends and make data-driven decisions in response to shifting consumer behaviors during the pandemic. It integrates multiple visualizations and KPIs to provide a holistic view of retail performance.

---

## Business Problem

The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated the shift from in-store to online shopping, creating challenges in understanding changing sales patterns across regions and product segments. Retailers needed a centralized tool to monitor sales performance and adapt strategies effectively. Without such insights, businesses risked missing opportunities and failing to respond to evolving customer preferences.

---

## Objective

- Create an interactive dashboard to visualize sales data by country, segment, category, and subcategory
- Enable identification of sales trends and performance variations across different dimensions
- Provide actionable insights to support data-driven decision making during market shifts

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel
- Power Query
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

## Project Workflow

- Gather and prepare sales data from multiple sources
- Build data model and relationships in Power BI
- Design and create visualizations including line and clustered column charts
- Develop KPIs and implement DAX calculations for sales indicators
- Publish and refine dashboard for stakeholder use

---

## Key Insights

- Sales patterns shifted significantly across countries during the pandemic
- Certain product categories and subcategories outperformed others in online sales
- Customer segments showed varying preferences impacting sales distribution
- Monthly sales trends revealed critical periods for targeted marketing efforts
