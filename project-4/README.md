
# Us Coffee Chain

## Project Overview

This project involved analyzing sales and profitability data for a US coffee chain selling 13 products across 4 product types in 20 states. Using Power BI and Tableau, various measures such as Sales Per Month, YOY%, and Gross Profit Margin were calculated to identify underperforming products and contributing factors. The analysis included drill-downs, filtering, and hierarchical data exploration to provide actionable insights.

---

## Business Problem

The coffee chain faced challenges in product profitability across multiple markets, with some products underperforming. Identifying the root causes and factors affecting profitability was critical to improving overall business performance.

---

## Objective

- Identify underperforming products and product types
- Analyze sales trends and profitability metrics over time
- Determine factors contributing to profitability issues

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Tableau
- DAX
- Excel
- Data Modeling
- Calculated Measures
- Filtering and Drill-down Techniques

---

## Project Workflow

- Load and understand sales data from Excel into Power BI
- Create calendar table and establish data relationships
- Develop DAX measures for sales and profitability metrics
- Perform high-level analysis and drill-downs using filtering and hierarchy
- Visualize insights with line and stacked column charts in Power BI

---

## Key Insights

- Certain products under specific product types consistently underperform in profitability
- Sales trends vary significantly across the 4 markets covering 20 states
- Gross Profit Margin fluctuates year-over-year, indicating seasonal or market-driven factors
- Detailed drill-downs reveal specific time periods and products contributing to profitability issues
