
# Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project involved creating an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze and visualize sales data by country, segment, category, and subcategory. It enabled stakeholders to understand sales trends and customer preferences during the digital shift accelerated by the pandemic. The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by consolidating key performance indicators and visual analytics.

---

## Business Problem

The global pandemic drastically changed consumer shopping behavior, increasing online purchases and complicating sales analysis across regions and product lines. Retailers needed a comprehensive tool to monitor and respond to these evolving sales patterns effectively. Without centralized insights, identifying growth opportunities and operational inefficiencies was challenging.

---

## Objective

- Create a dynamic dashboard to visualize sales by country, segment, category, and subcategory
- Enable analysis of sales trends and customer behavior during the pandemic
- Provide actionable insights to support strategic retail decisions

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

## Project Workflow

- Gather and prepare sales data from multiple sources
- Build data model and relationships in Power BI
- Design and create key visualizations including line and clustered column charts
- Develop KPIs and interactive filters such as date slicers
- Assemble and finalize the dashboard for stakeholder use

---

## Key Insights

- Sales trends varied significantly by country during the pandemic period
- Certain product categories and subcategories showed increased online demand
- Customer segments responded differently to the shift towards e-shopping
- Interactive KPIs and visuals improved visibility into monthly sales performance
