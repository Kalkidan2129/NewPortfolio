
# Us Coffee Chain

## Project Overview

This project involved analyzing sales and profitability data of a US coffee chain selling 13 products across 4 product types in 20 states. Using Power BI and Tableau, key metrics such as Sales Per Month, YOY%, and Gross Profit Margin were calculated to identify underperforming products and contributing factors. The analysis provided detailed insights through drill-downs, filtering, and calculated measures to support strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

The coffee chain faced challenges in understanding the profitability issues of its diverse product portfolio across multiple markets. Identifying which products were underperforming and the factors affecting profitability was critical to improving overall business performance. The lack of clear insights hindered targeted actions to enhance sales and margins.

---

## Objective

- Identify underperforming products and product types impacting profitability
- Analyze sales trends and profitability metrics across markets and time periods
- Provide actionable insights to improve product performance and profitability

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Tableau
- DAX
- Excel
- Data Modeling
- Calculated Measures
- Filtering and Drill-Down Techniques

---

## Project Workflow

- Load and understand sales data from Excel into Power BI
- Create calendar and data model relationships for time-based analysis
- Develop DAX measures for key metrics like Sales Per Month and GPM%
- Use filtering, drill-down, and hierarchy features to explore data
- Build interactive reports highlighting underperforming products and causes

---

## Key Insights

- Certain products within coffee and tea categories consistently underperform in profitability
- Sales trends vary significantly across the 4 markets and 20 states
- Gross Profit Margin fluctuates year-over-year indicating seasonal or market-driven impacts
- High-level and detailed drill-down analysis reveals specific factors contributing to product underperformance
