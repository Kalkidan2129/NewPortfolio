
# Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project involved creating an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze global superstore sales data by country, segment, category, and subcategory. It visualizes key sales metrics and trends to support data-driven business decisions. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance and identify growth opportunities effectively.

---

## Business Problem

The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated the shift to online shopping, changing consumer behavior and sales patterns globally. Retailers needed a comprehensive tool to visualize and understand sales performance across multiple dimensions to adapt strategies. Without clear insights, businesses risked missing opportunities and failing to respond to market changes.

---

## Objective

- Create a dynamic dashboard to visualize sales by country, segment, category, and subcategory
- Enable identification of sales trends and key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Support data-driven decision-making for retail sales optimization

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

## Project Workflow

- Gather and prepare global superstore sales data
- Build data model and relationships in Power BI
- Create visualizations including line and clustered column charts
- Develop KPIs and sales indicators using DAX calculations
- Assemble and finalize the interactive sales dashboard

---

## Key Insights

- Sales performance varies significantly by country and segment
- Certain product categories and subcategories drive majority of revenue
- Pandemic influenced a shift towards increased online sales
- Visualizing sales trends monthly highlights growth and decline periods
