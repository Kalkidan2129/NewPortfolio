
# Us Coffee Chain

## Project Overview

This project involved analyzing sales and profitability data for a US coffee chain selling 13 products across 4 product types in 20 states. Using Power BI and Tableau, various measures such as Sales Per Month, YOY%, and Gross Profit Margin were calculated to identify underperforming products and contributing factors. The analysis included data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive visualizations to deliver actionable business insights.

---

## Business Problem

The coffee chain faced challenges in identifying which products were underperforming and understanding the factors impacting profitability. Without clear insights, optimizing product offerings and improving margins was difficult. The project aimed to uncover these issues through detailed data analysis.

---

## Objective

- Identify underperforming products impacting profitability
- Analyze sales trends and gross profit margins over time
- Provide actionable insights to improve product performance

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Tableau
- DAX
- Excel
- Power BI Desktop
- Data Modeling
- Calculated Measures
- Filtering and Drill-down Techniques

---

## Project Workflow

- Load and understand sales data from Excel into Power BI
- Create calendar table and establish data relationships
- Develop DAX measures for sales and profitability metrics
- Build interactive visualizations with drill-down and filtering
- Analyze results to identify underperforming products and causes

---

## Key Insights

- Certain products under specific product types showed consistent underperformance
- Sales and gross profit margins varied significantly across the 4 markets
- Year-over-year sales trends revealed declining performance in some product categories
- Profitability issues were linked to specific products and market segments
