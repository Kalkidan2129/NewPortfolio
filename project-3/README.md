
# Us Coffee Chain

## Project Overview

This project involved analyzing sales and profitability data for a US coffee chain across 20 states and 4 markets. Using Power BI and Tableau, the analysis included creating calculated measures such as Sales Per Month, YOY%, and GPM% to uncover product performance issues. The final report identified underperforming products and the factors impacting their profitability.

---

## Business Problem

The coffee chain faced challenges in understanding the profitability of its diverse product portfolio across multiple markets. There was a need to identify which products were underperforming and the reasons behind their low profitability to inform business decisions.

---

## Objective

- Analyze sales and profitability metrics across products and markets
- Identify underperforming products and contributing factors
- Provide actionable insights to improve product profitability

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Tableau
- DAX
- Excel
- Power BI Desktop
- Data Modeling
- Calculated Measures
- Filtering and Drill-down Techniques

---

## Project Workflow

- Load and prepare data in Power BI Desktop
- Create calendar table and establish data relationships
- Develop calculated DAX measures for key metrics
- Perform high-level analysis with drill-downs and filtering
- Visualize data using line and stacked column charts to identify trends

---

## Key Insights

- Certain product types like espresso and herbal tea showed lower profitability trends
- Sales performance varied significantly across the four markets and 20 states
- Year-over-year growth percentages highlighted declining sales in specific products
- Gross profit margin analysis revealed key factors impacting product profitability
