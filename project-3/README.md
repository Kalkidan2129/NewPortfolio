
# Power BI Dashboard of US Accidents: 2016 - 2020

## Project Overview

This project analyzed US traffic accident data from 2016 to 2020 across all 50 states using Power BI. It visualized trends such as accident frequency, severity, timing, and weather conditions to identify risk factors. The dashboard supports data-driven decisions to improve road safety and reduce fatalities.

---

## Business Problem

Road traffic crashes are a leading cause of death in the US, with millions injured and over 90 daily fatalities. Despite fewer vehicles on roads during COVID-19, fatal accidents increased due to reckless driving. There is a critical need for data insights to inform policies and public awareness to reduce accidents and save lives.

---

## Objective

- Analyze accident trends by day, month, and hour to identify safer travel times
- Assess year-over-year changes in accident severity and frequency
- Evaluate weather and other factors contributing to accident risks nationwide

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI Desktop
- Power BI Service
- National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) data
- ETL processes
- Data mining techniques

---

## Project Workflow

- Collect and clean US accident data from 2016-2020
- Perform exploratory data analysis to identify key trends
- Develop interactive Power BI dashboard with multiple visualizations
- Publish dashboard to Power BI Service for public sharing
- Embed and share dashboard link with stakeholders

---

## Key Insights

- Fatal accident percentages increased during COVID-19 despite fewer vehicles on roads
- Reckless driving significantly contributed to rising fatality rates
- Certain times of day and months show higher accident frequencies
- Weather conditions play a notable role in accident severity nationwide
