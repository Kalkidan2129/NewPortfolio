
# Adidas US Sales Dashboard in Power BI

## Project Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to analyze Adidas USA sales performance between 2020 and 2021. Key performance indicators such as total sales, monthly sales, operating profit, and profit margins were visualized to provide actionable business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor sales trends and profitability to support data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Adidas needed to understand its sales and profit performance in the US market to identify growth opportunities and areas for improvement. With increasing competition, especially from Nike, analyzing sales data was critical to maintain market position and enhance profitability. The challenge was to consolidate and visualize complex sales metrics effectively for strategic decision-making.

---

## Objective

- Analyze Adidas USA sales and profit trends from 2020 to 2021
- Visualize key performance indicators including sales, operating profit, and margins
- Enable data-driven insights to support business growth and operational improvements

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel
- Microsoft Office
- Kaggle Dataset

---

## Project Workflow

- Import sales data into Power BI from Kaggle dataset
- Clean and transform data using Power Query Editor
- Create calendar and refresh date tables for time intelligence
- Build data model by establishing relationships between tables
- Develop DAX measures and design the final dashboard for visualization

---

## Key Insights

- Monthly sales trends reveal seasonal fluctuations impacting revenue
- Operating profit margins showed month-over-month variations indicating cost management opportunities
- Adidas surpassed Nike in US customer satisfaction in 2021, reflecting strong brand positioning
- Sales and profit data highlight key periods for targeted marketing and inventory planning
